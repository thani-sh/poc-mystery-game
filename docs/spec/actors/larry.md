# Laurence (Larry) - Character Description

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Laurence "Larry" Daykin
- **Setting**: 1940s-1950s UK (Peterswood)
- **Age**: 12-13 years old (One of the elder members)

## Physical Features

- Average height and build for his age.
- Brown hair, kept relatively short and neat.
- Friendly, open face.
- Looks like a responsible older brother type.

## Clothing and Appearance

- **Casual Holiday Wear**: Collared shirts (checked or plain), knitted jumpers (pullovers), shorts, and knee socks.
- **Outerwear**: Simple jacket or macintosh.
- **Accessories**: None specific, perhaps a notebook.
- **General Vibe**: Sensible, ordinary, and reliable.

## Character-Specific Notes

- **Personality**: Level-headed, fair, and responsible. He was the leader before Fatty arrived and accepted Fatty's leadership gracefully.
- **Role**: Often acts as the deputy leader, organizing the others when Fatty is busy or in disguise.
- **Key Expression**: Serious determination or a friendly grin.
- **Dislikes**: Unfairness, people being mean to the younger ones.
