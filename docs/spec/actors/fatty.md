# Frederick (Fatty) - Character Description

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Frederick Algernon "Fatty" Trotteville
- **Setting**: 1940s-1950s UK (Peterswood)
- **Age**: 12-13 years old (The leader of the Five Find-Outers)

## Physical Features

- Stout build, living up to his nickname, though he claims it is "muscles".
- Dark hair, often slicked back or neat.
- Intelligent, sharp eyes that twinkle with mischief.
- Confident posture, often standing with hands in pockets or gesturing expansively.

## Clothing and Appearance

- **Casual Holiday Wear**: Smart blazer, crisp shirt, tie (often loose), grey flannel shorts or trousers.
- **Outerwear**: Expensive-looking coat or raincoat.
- **Accessories**: Often carries disguise kits, greasepaint, or props.
- **General Vibe**: Well-to-do, slightly pompous but undeniably capable.

## Character-Specific Notes

- **Personality**: Boastful, brilliant, generous, and a natural leader. He loves to show off his skills in disguise, ventriloquism, and deduction.
- **Role**: The mastermind of the group. He solves the mysteries and often outwits Mr. Goon.
- **Key Expression**: Smug satisfaction or intense concentration.
- **Dislikes**: Being doubted, Mr. Goon (though he enjoys teasing him).
