# Buster - Character Description

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Buster
- **Setting**: 1940s-1950s UK (Peterswood)
- **Species**: Dog (Scottish Terrier / Scottie)

## Physical Features

- Small, sturdy black dog.
- Shaggy fur, especially around the face (beard and eyebrows).
- Bright, intelligent eyes visible under the fur.
- Short legs, perky tail.

## Clothing and Appearance

- **Accessories**: A smart leather collar.
- **General Vibe**: Energetic, fierce (for his size), and loyal.

## Character-Specific Notes

- **Personality**: Extremely loyal to Fatty and the other children. He hates Mr. Goon and loves to nip at his ankles. He is brave and has a good nose for clues.
- **Role**: The team's mascot and protector. He often finds things the children miss or creates a distraction.
- **Key Expression**: Alert ears, bared teeth (at Goon), or happy panting.
- **Dislikes**: Mr. Goon (intensely), cats.
