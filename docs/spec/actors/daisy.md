# Margaret (Daisy) - Character Description

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Margaret "Daisy" Daykin
- **Setting**: 1940s-1950s UK (Peterswood)
- **Age**: 11-12 years old (Larry's sister)

## Physical Features

- Similar features to Larry, clearly siblings.
- Hair often tied back or in plaits/braids, neat and tidy.
- Bright, intelligent eyes.
- Often smiling or looking thoughtful.

## Clothing and Appearance

- **Casual Holiday Wear**: Summer dresses with floral patterns or simple blouses and skirts.
- **Outerwear**: Cardigans, light coats.
- **Accessories**: Hair ribbons, maybe a small bag.
- **General Vibe**: Practical, kind, and ready to help.

## Character-Specific Notes

- **Personality**: Sensible, creative, and observant. She is good at thinking of ideas that the boys might miss.
- **Role**: A core member of the team, often working with Bets or Larry. She is supportive and smart.
- **Key Expression**: Thoughtful frown or a bright smile of realization.
- **Dislikes**: Being underestimated.
