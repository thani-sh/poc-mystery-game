# Elizabeth (Bets) - Character Description

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Elizabeth "Bets" Hilton
- **Setting**: 1940s-1950s UK (Peterswood)
- **Age**: 8-9 years old (The youngest of the Five Find-Outers)

## Physical Features

- Small stature, being the youngest of the group
- Fresh, youthful face with bright, observant eyes
- Typically depicted with shoulder-length or bobbed hair (often fair or light brown in illustrations)
- Expressive face that easily shows admiration or concern

## Clothing and Appearance

- **Casual Holiday Wear**: 1940s/50s style summer dresses with puffed sleeves, pinafores, or simple blouse and skirt combinations.
- **Outerwear**: Knitted cardigans, knee-length socks, and sturdy shoes (sandals or t-bar shoes).
- **Accessories**: Occasionally ribbons in hair.
- **General Vibe**: Neat but active, ready for playing and investigating, though often "tagged along" by the older children.

## Character-Specific Notes

- **Personality**: Kind-hearted, loyal, and keenly observant. She hero-worships Fatty (Frederick Trotteville) and is his staunchest defender.
- **Role**: Often notices small details that the older children miss. She is the "heart" of the group, often smoothing over conflicts or comforting others.
- **Key Expression**: Look of adoration or wide-eyed wonder when Fatty displays a new disguise or skill.
- **Dislikes**: Being left out because she is "too young".
