# Theophilus Goon (Mr. Goon) - Character Description

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Theophilus Goon
- **Setting**: 1940s-1950s UK (Peterswood)
- **Role**: Village Policeman (Constable)

## Physical Features

- Large, imposing, and heavy-set.
- Red face, which gets redder when he is angry.
- Bulging eyes (often described as "goggling").
- Large feet (often tripped over by Buster).

## Clothing and Appearance

- **Uniform**: Standard 1940s/50s British police uniform.
  - Dark blue tunic with silver buttons.
  - Belt.
  - Tall custodian helmet (often knocked off).
  - Heavy black boots.
- **General Vibe**: Pompous, blustering, and often flustered.

## Character-Specific Notes

- **Personality**: Unpleasant, bullying, and incompetent. He considers the Five Find-Outers to be "pests" and is constantly trying to "clear them orf". He is jealous of their success.
- **Role**: The main antagonist (though not a villain). He tries to solve the mysteries before the children but usually fails.
- **Key Expression**: Angry glare, purple-faced rage, or suspicious squint.
- **Catchphrase**: "Clear Orf!"
- **Dislikes**: The Five Find-Outers, Buster, Inspector Jenks (when he's being told off), being outsmarted.
