# Philip (Pip) - Character Description

## Basic Information

- **Name**: Philip "Pip" Hilton
- **Setting**: 1940s-1950s UK (Peterswood)
- **Age**: 11-12 years old (Bets' brother)

## Physical Features

- Boyish, slightly scruffy hair (often needs a cut).
- Often has a slightly exasperated look (usually directed at Bets).
- Energetic stance.

## Clothing and Appearance

- **Casual Holiday Wear**: Shirts (often with sleeves rolled up), shorts, scuffed knees.
- **Outerwear**: Jumpers, rough-and-tumble clothes.
- **Accessories**: None specific.
- **General Vibe**: Active, impatient, typical schoolboy.

## Character-Specific Notes

- **Personality**: Sometimes impatient, especially with his younger sister Bets, but ultimately protective. He is eager to solve mysteries but can be a bit hasty.
- **Role**: Active investigator, often paired with Larry.
- **Key Expression**: Frown of concentration or annoyance, or excitement.
- **Dislikes**: Bets being "babyish", Mr. Goon.
