# Mr. Goon — Theophilus Goon (Adult — Village Constable)

**Personality:** Pompous, bullying, often flustered and jealous of the Find-Outers.

**Appearance:** Traditional 1940s/50s constable uniform (dark tunic, custodian helmet, heavy boots); red-faced when angry.

**Visual Cues:** Blustering poses; expressions: angry glare or flustered indignation.
