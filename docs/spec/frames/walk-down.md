# Walk Down

## Description

Walking down animation - walking toward viewer, left foot forward.

## Pose Details

- Character walking toward the viewer (front view)
- Left foot forward, right foot back
- We see their front fully, not sides
- Arms swinging naturally (right arm forward when left leg forward)
- Body has slight forward lean indicating movement
- Weight on front foot
