# Garden Tileset

Generate a tileset for a garden environment in the town of Peterswood.

## Environment Description

A charming English country garden typical of a small 1950s village. The garden should include:

- **Ground Tiles**: Various grass types (short lawn grass, slightly longer grass, worn grass paths), dirt patches, gravel paths, paved stone walkways
- **Garden Beds**: Flower beds with colorful blooms (roses, daisies, tulips), vegetable patches with visible crops (tomatoes, carrots, lettuce)
- **Water Features**: Small pond tiles (center, edges, corners), fountain tiles
- **Decorative Elements**: Garden borders, hedges (short and tall), white picket fence sections (straight, corners), wooden garden benches, bird baths, sundials
- **Vegetation**: Bush tiles, small tree trunks, tree canopy tiles, climbing roses on trellises
- **Transitions**: Smooth transition tiles between grass and dirt, grass and path, path and flower beds

## Specific Tiles to Include

Include tiles that can be combined to create:

- Open lawn areas
- Winding garden paths
- Enclosed garden beds
- Water features
- Seating areas
- Decorative borders and fencing

Ensure tiles can seamlessly connect to create larger continuous areas and natural-looking boundaries.

## Style Notes

- Maintain the 1950s English countryside aesthetic
- Use warm, inviting colors typical of a well-maintained garden
- Keep the retro JRPG art style consistent with character designs
- Ensure all tiles work from a top-down perspective
- Design for clarity at small tile sizes (16x16 pixels per tile)
